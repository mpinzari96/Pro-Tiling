Bathroom Tile Services We Provide

Bathroom Floor Tile

A new bathroom floor completely changes the feel of the space. We install everything from classic penny round mosaic in a master bath to large 24x48 porcelain in a modern primary suite. Heated floor systems, linear drains, and waterproofed wet room designs are all within our wheelhouse.

Bathroom Wall Tile

Wall tile in a bathroom can go floor-to-ceiling or run as wainscoting — either way we plan the layout to ensure balanced cuts, level courses, and grout lines that actually align from wall to wall. A lot of DIY and rushed contractor work fails here. Ours doesn't.

Tub Surrounds

A properly waterproofed and tiled tub surround extends the life of the installation and eliminates water intrusion issues. We install shower-grade waterproofing membranes on all wet areas — not just cement board — before any tile goes up.

Accent & Decorative Tile

Listello borders, mosaic feature strips, niche tile, and decorative field tile transitions are where a bathroom goes from nice to exceptional. We work closely with you during planning to incorporate accent details that pull the room together.

What to Expect During Your Bathroom Tile Remodel

We know you're letting people into your home. We keep the work area contained, protect surrounding surfaces, and clean up every day before we leave. Here's how a typical bathroom tile remodel unfolds with Pro Tiling:

  • Initial consultation and free estimate (usually same week)
  • Material and tile selection guidance if needed
  • Demo of existing tile and surface assessment
  • Waterproofing installation on all wet areas
  • Tile setting — floor first, walls second for best results
  • Curing time, then grout and sealant
  • Final cleanup and walkthrough — you approve before we leave

Popular Bathroom Tile Styles in Sacramento

Sacramento homeowners are increasingly moving toward large-format porcelain for primary bathrooms — it reads as more luxurious and reduces grout maintenance. For secondary bathrooms and powder rooms, subway tile in a variety of finishes and patterns remains the most popular choice. We'll show you what's trending and what actually works for your space during your estimate.
