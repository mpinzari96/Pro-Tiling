Waterproofing is the most important part of a tiled shower system.

## What should be included
A complete system includes substrate prep, waterproof membrane, sealed transitions, and proper flood testing before tile is set.

## Warning signs in contractor proposals
If the estimate is vague about waterproofing materials or testing, ask for specifics before approving work.
