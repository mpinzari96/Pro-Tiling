Backsplashes in 2026 are leaning clean, textured, and intentional.

## Leading styles
Vertical stack bond, handmade-look ceramic, and full-height backsplash installations continue to grow.

## Keep it timeless
Choose a durable neutral field tile, then bring personality through grout tone or hardware accents.
