Our Story

Started in Sacramento. Stayed in Sacramento.

Pro Tiling started as a straightforward idea: hire a tile contractor who actually takes pride in the work. That sounds basic — but anyone who's dealt with contractors who disappear mid-job, leave grout residue on the tile, or cut corners on waterproofing knows it's rarer than it should be.

Over the past 10+ years we've built a reputation across Sacramento, Elk Grove, Roseville, and Folsom for showing up on time, communicating clearly, and delivering work that genuinely exceeds what clients expected. Our business has grown almost entirely through referrals — which is the only growth metric that actually matters to us.

We specialize in residential tile work because we believe depth of focus produces better results than breadth of services. Tile is all we do, so tile is what we do best.

How We Work

The Standards We Hold Ourselves To

Every job we take on, no matter the size, gets our full attention. These are the principles that guide how we work.

Waterproofing First

We never skip or shortchange the waterproofing step. It's not visible in the final result — but it's the difference between a shower that lasts 20 years and one that fails in 3.

Honest Estimates

We give you a detailed, written estimate before any work begins. If scope changes during the project, we tell you before we do the work — not after.

Clean Job Sites

We protect surrounding surfaces, contain the work area, and clean up every day before we leave. Your home doesn't become a construction site just because we're in it.

Precision Over Speed

We're not the fastest crew in Sacramento — we're the most careful. Layout planning, consistent joint spacing, and perfectly cut edges take time. That time shows in the finished product.

Direct Communication

When you call Pro Tiling, you talk to us. Not a dispatcher, not an answering service. We keep you informed throughout the project and respond quickly to questions.

We Stand Behind Our Work

If something isn't right after we leave, we come back and make it right. No runaround. That's a simple promise, and we keep it.
